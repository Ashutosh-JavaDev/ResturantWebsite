Our Story

Where Tradition Meets Innovation

Since 2012, Blackwood has been a cornerstone of Manhattan's culinary landscape. We believe in the power of ingredients sourced at their peak, prepared with precision, and served with genuine hospitality.

Our kitchen operates on the principles of classical French technique refined through decades of experience, yet we remain constantly inspired by the diverse flavors that make New York unique.

Executive Chef

Marcus Chen

Chef Marcus Chen brings over twenty years of culinary expertise to Blackwood, having trained under luminaries in Paris, Tokyo, and New York. His approach combines classical technique with a modern sensibility, always letting the ingredients lead.

Before joining Blackwood, Chef Chen earned a Michelin star at Lumière in San Francisco and served as Sous Chef at Le Bernardin. His philosophy is simple: respect the ingredient, honor the season, and serve with integrity.
